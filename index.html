Louis Theroux

A picture of Louis Theroux posing with his hand on his cheek

Louis theroux was born on the 20th of May 1970 in Singapore to an English mother and American father (travel writer Paul Theroux). Theroux moved with his family to England at the age of one and was raised in London. Louis first worked as a journalist for Metro Silicon Valley an alternative free weekly newspaper in San Jose, California. In 1992 he was hired for Spy magazine. His first break in TV was on Michael Moore’s TV Nation series. After TV Nation ended he signed a deal with BBC and began to make Louis Theroux’s Weird Weekends and other documentaries for the BBC after this ended. This is how most people today know him and he has gone on to make over 60+ documentaries on various topics.
